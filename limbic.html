The Limbic System!

Below the cerebral hemispheres.
Associated with drives and emotions.

Hippocampus

Processes conscious/ explicit memories.

Losing the hippocampus means losing the ability to form new memories on events and facts! (anterograde amnesia!!)

Amygdala

Little lima bean sized structures.
Linked with aggression and fear.

Pituitary Gland

The "master gland" or "control pit."

Dictates what the endocrine system does. (hormones)

Hypothalamus

"Below" the thalamus.

Controls maintenance activities such as eating, drinking, and body temperature.
Oversees the pituatary gland's control of the endocrine system.
Associated with emotions and rewards!
